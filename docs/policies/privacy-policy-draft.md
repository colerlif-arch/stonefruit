# Privacy Policy (Draft for Go-Live)

Status: Draft (not yet published)  
Last updated: February 15, 2026

## 1. Overview

Stonefruit is a personal analytics service that processes user-authorized data to generate financial and contextual insights.

## 2. Data We Collect

Depending on enabled features, Stonefruit may collect:
- Financial account and transaction data (via Plaid, with user authorization)
- Optional contextual data (for example location data and photo metadata)
- Technical and operational logs required to run and secure the service

## 3. How We Use Data

We use data to:
- Ingest and normalize source records
- Produce deterministic derivations and summaries
- Generate user-requested outputs and prompts
- Maintain security and reliability

## 4. Data Sharing

Stonefruit does not sell personal data.  
Data is shared only as required to provide the service (for example, with infrastructure or API providers acting as service providers/processors).

## 5. Access Tokens and Credentials

- Plaid access tokens are stored server-side only.
- Credentials are not exposed to client applications.
- Exposed credentials/tokens are revoked and rotated.

## 6. Retention and Deletion

Stonefruit follows a documented Data Retention and Disposal Policy.  
Data is retained only as long as necessary for service and compliance needs, then securely disposed.

## 7. Security

Stonefruit uses administrative, technical, and operational safeguards to protect data, including access control, secret management, and incident response procedures.

## 8. Your Rights

Where applicable law grants rights to access, correction, deletion, or portability, Stonefruit will provide mechanisms to honor those rights.

## 9. International and Jurisdictional Notes

Applicable privacy rights and obligations vary by jurisdiction. Stonefruit will apply required legal standards based on user location and governing law.

## 10. Contact

Privacy requests and questions should be directed to the Stonefruit operator at go-live contact channels.
